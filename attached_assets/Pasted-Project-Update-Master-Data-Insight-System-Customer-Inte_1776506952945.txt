The system shall be enhanced with a Master Data Insight System that retrieves customer, billing, and sales data from POS and internal modules, processes it using date-driven analytics, and generates insights such as customer visit patterns, purchase behaviour, peak hours, comparative trends, and business intelligence metrics. This module shall act as the central intelligence layer of the cafe system and enable data-driven decision-making.
